ABOUT

I'm a UI designer based in Naples, FL. I have a background in health care and UI design and have been working as a caregiver for the past 3 years. Combining my background in all, I am well equipped with various skills such as conducting research, problem-solving, and creating products with consumers in mind. I love to design and create things.
